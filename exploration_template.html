Motivation :¶

Based on the given dataset, one would like to determine the usage of bike rentals by the time/hours of the day or by the day of the week to find out which hours of the day or which days of the week are busy versus the ones which are not busy. One would also like to know the busiest places for bike rentals.Going deeper one may like to find out the usage of bike rentals by each gender or by different age groups. Dataset can also be used to find out the number of subscribers versus the customers in general or for each gender.
